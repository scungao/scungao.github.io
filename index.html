Sicun (Sean) Gao

Postdoctoral Researcher
MIT CSAIL (Office: 32-G770)
Email: sicung at csail dot mit dot edu

I combine logical/algebraic/numerical approaches to develop automated and reliable methods for the design/analysis/security of cyber-physical systems. I'm working with Armando Solar-Lezama at MIT, after spending quite some time with Edmund Clarke and Jeremy Avigad at CMU.
Software
  • dReal [tool page] [try online]
    SMT solver for nonlinear theories over the reals, allowing transcendental functions and differential equations. It has scaled on formulas with hundreds of nonlinear ODEs.
  • dReach [tool page]
    Reachability analysis and planning tool for nonlinear hybrid systems using bounded model checking and invariant-based reasoning.
