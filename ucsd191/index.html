Undergraduate Thesis Research Seminar (Fall 2021)

  • Instructor: Sicun Gao
  • The seminar provides guidance for undergraduate students who aim to pursue independent research in computer science and engineering. It is intended for students who have some research experience, know that they enjoy doing research, and are planning to apply for PhD programs. We will discuss various aspects of academic research relevant for junior researchers, and help students get started or make progress on an honors thesis topic in their areas of interest. Application for enrollment is required through this form.
